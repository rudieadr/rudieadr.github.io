Long Term Stays

Here at Temple Inn & Suites, we are pleased to offer options for long-term stay if you are a Temple Missionaries or Patrons.

Our room options include:

  • Kitchenettes for all room types
  • Handicapped options for some room types
  • All other free amenities included with rooms

Rooms Available (Please note: this is not a guarantee of availability. Please contact us for rooms/dates available)

One Bedroom Suite

  • 550 - 680 sq. ft.
  • 1 king or queen bed with 1 pull-out sofa
  • Occupancy: 2-4 adults

Suitable for a family traveling with kids or a Temple Missionary Couple. Each room comes with a stove, microwave, fridge, and ice-maker. All rooms are ADA-compatible

Two Bedroom Suite

  • 800 sq. ft.
  • Room Options: 1 king or 2 doubles in the guest room
  • Occupancy: 4-8 adults

Suitable for a large family. Each room comes with a stove, microwave, fridge, and ice-maker. All rooms are ADA-compatible
