Cats Galore

By Ze Old Cat Lady

LOCAL NEWS: A Cat Extravaganza is taking place in Preston this weekend.

Cats Protection Preston Branch is holding its annual event at St Leonard’s Church Hall in Marshall’s Brow, Penwortham.

The Extravaganza will include a raffle draw, tombola, ID disc engraving and refreshments.

Stalls will be selling new cat-themed gifts and housewares, donated goods, crafts and toiletries. Also available will be Cats Protection Christmas cards, plus 2020 diaries and calendars.

Judging of the annual pet photography competition will take place, and there will be the chance to find out more about volunteering for Cats Protection Preston Branch.
