Temple Inn & Suites

Hotel Locations



Our Inn & Suites is dedicated to providing accommodations and services for Temple patrons. If your Ward is here for a Temple visit or your family is doing a Temple wedding, we are here to make sure your stay is comfortable for as long as needed.



The Memphis Tennessee Temple opened on April 23, 2000 with the ground breaking dedication done on January 16, 1999. The temple sits next to the Bartlett, TN stake center and services more than 20,000 members from Tennessee, Arkansas, Missouri, and Mississippi. The total floor area for this temple is 10,700 sq ft with two ordinance and two sealing rooms.
